Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Below are some commonly asked questions regarding the services offered by Cribs

How long do the designs take ?

The duration varies greatly depending on the type of project at hand. However, Cribs strives to complete the designs as agreed upon with the client.

Are the designs mine once I purchase them or Cribs can sell them to another client ?

Once purchased, the designs are solely yours and Cribs cannot resell them to another client or use them in any of their projects. Read more on Legal Terms & Conditions

Do I need to pay for consultation ?

No! Our consultation/enquiry services are free

Do I pay for the design and construction work seperately ?

Yes! The design and construction work is independent of each other and you'll be expected to pay for the two seperately.

What if I already have my own design ?

In that case, Cribs will only charge you for construction (or your desired service). We charge you only for what you requested.

When do I pay for the design(s) ?

A 50% down payment must be made before Cribs can start working on the design(s). The remaining 50% is paid upon completetion of the design(s). See our Payment Options.

Can I request a refund if I change my mind ?

This depends on how much of the design has been completed. If a refund is requested before Cribs has completed 50% of the design, it can be issued. If Cribs has already completed more than 50% of the design, a refund cannot be issued. Read more on Terms of Service.

How do I get a Loan ?

You have to come through to any of our branch offices and present a National Identity Card and Bank Documents.

Note: All our loans are collateral based. Be informed also that we only issue loans in person. See our loan plans and the types of collateral we accept here.
